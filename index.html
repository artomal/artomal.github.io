HealthBridge

Bridge the health data your Fitbit collects into Apple Health — automatically, privately, entirely on your device.

How it works

Connect your Google account once, pick the metrics you want — sleep, heart rate, HRV, SpO₂ and more — and HealthBridge keeps Apple Health in sync in the background. Read-only against Google, no servers, no duplicates.
